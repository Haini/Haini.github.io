Remove a package in Debian

Posted on October 9, 2016

If you just downgraded from Debian/sid to Debian/main like I did you may have a ton of broken dependencies. Mainly from manually added PPAs, in my case from the TinyOS one. I didn’t do any TinyOS development lately and so I couldn’t care less about these packages that were keeping me from my desired downgrade.

If you don’t care about broken dependencies for a package because you know you won’t use it ever again and you are in a hurry - here is the solution: 

sudo dpkg --force-all -P <youFavouriteDependentPackage>
