Run Matlab from desktop shell

Posted on September 16, 2016

As the title says I installed Matlab R2015b recently and instead of launching it up from terminal (which seems to be the default) I wanted to launch it from my also newly installed Gnome desktop (I switched over from dwm recently and installed Matlab before).

A *.desktop file was already created in ~/.local/share/applications/matlab.desktop, but the Exec key didnâ€™t hold the argument "-desktop".

Long story short - just put Exec=matlab "-desktop" in your .desktop file and you are good to go.
